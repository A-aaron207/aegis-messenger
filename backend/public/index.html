🛡️ What is Aegis?

Aegis is an ultra-secure, decentralized E2EE messenger designed to establish absolute privacy for professional and personal workspaces.

By removing single points of failure, Aegis enables you to communicate, share files, and place voice calls without relying on traditional centralized trusted intermediaries.

🔑 Zero-Knowledge Data Management

Your encryption keys are derived completely client-side using industry-standard Web Crypto APIs (AES-GCM-256 and ECDH).

The server acts as a blind relayer: it never holds or receives your plaintext passwords, decryption keys, file content, or call streams. Everything is encrypted before leaving your physical device.

📞 Decentralized P2P Voice Calling

Establish voice call connections using WebRTC direct peer-to-peer data channels. No phone numbers, cellular plans, or registrations are ever required.

Aegis negotiates E2EE voice streams through cryptographic invitations. ICE candidate routing runs through blind relays to establish direct caller-to-recipient E2EE voice tunnels.

💬 Respectful Workspace Compliance

Aegis values high-grade, professional alignment. An intelligent, zero-knowledge content filter runs strictly inside your local browser container:

  • Abusive Content Warnings: Prompts on harsh profanities, with bypass or cancel options.
  • Gen-Z Slang Hard-Deny: Blocks unprofessional slang (e.g. rizz, skibidi, gyatt) to uphold workspace integrity.
